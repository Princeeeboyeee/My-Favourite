My Favourite Hobby

Hockey

About

Hockey is a fun and entertaining game to play. I love to play hockey anytime, normally I play games on Saturday and train on monday. Hockey is an awesome sport because it makes you feel like you want to keep playing and have fun, hockey is a sport with a lot of running so you will be really fit if you keep training and playing. Hockey is not that hard to learn, it doesn't have many rules which you might forget the rules and commit a foul. Therefore this is my favourite hobby, hockey is a sport that shows teamwork, fitness and happiness.
